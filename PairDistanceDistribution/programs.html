    Incomplete list of SAS software packages that can calculate p(r):
  • BayesApp (using the BIFT algorithm)
  • SasView
  • SASFit
  • BioXTAS Raw (using the BIFT or GNOM algorithms)
  • ATSAS (using the GNOM algorithm)
Moreover, CaPP can calculate the p(r) from a protein data bank (PDB) file and Shape2SAS can calculate the p(r) from a user-defined shape.
